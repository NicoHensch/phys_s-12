Reading

Read Chapters 1-3 of Making Things Move. Make a note of some of the more interesting points you learn.

Interesting things I learned in chapters 1-3: An interesting thing I learned while reading was that 3 points make up the minimum contraint design while 4 is too many. Having too many points can often lead to problems, hence why 4-legged tables wobble. However, 3 legged tables can still fall over. That is why we use 4 wheels for cars since they won't tip over, making the 4th wheel a redundant constraint since it is not requiered, but still very helpful.
