Inspiration

I chose to make a remote controlled car for a very simple reason. I like cars. More specifically, over the past couple of years I have become fascinated with formula 1, a fast paced racing championship. Although it may appear to be just some cars driving around in circles very quickly, there is an incredible amount of engineering that occurs behind the scenes. New ideas on aerodynamics, bio feuls, reusable energy, and safety are all being pioneered by skilled engineers. It is because of this that I have been drawn to engineering to begin with and it only seemed fitting to make my final project have something to do with F1. Although, my RC car can not go 250 mph, or looks identitical to an F1 car, it was still an incredibly challenging project for a newbie engineer like me.
